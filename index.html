Lowcountry Hackathons

What is a harborHack
and why have it?

Charlestonhacks harborHack is a dynamic event where diverse individuals collaborate to innovate in a fast-paced and creative environment, unleashing problem-solving skills to develop innovative solutions to real-world problems. It fosters community, out-of-the-box thinking, and celebrates the power of human ingenuity to make a positive impact. "Charlestonhacks" is open to hackers from all different backgrounds and skill levels, including developers, designers, business professionals, subject matter experts, designers, entrepreneurs, and students with an interest in technology, innovation, and social impact.

Charlestonhacks organizes Hackathons and other events to support our community. Our mission is to turn Charleston into a technology and creative hub, creating a pipeline of motivated technologists and creatives to participate in hackathon events (novice to expert), and retain their talent in Charleston South Carolina.

Charleston Summer Hack

The Charleston Summer Hacks are a community run hackathon in Charleston, South Carolina, and co-hosted by the Charleston Digital Corridor at the Charleston Tech Center. A 5-hour in-person hackathon focused on fostering innovation and collaboration to contribute to the growth of Charleston, South Carolina as a thriving technology hub.

HarborHack

The harbor hack will be a community run hackathon in Charleston, South Carolina, and co-hosted by the College of Charleston at the Charleston harbor Center. A weekend long in-person hackathon focused on fostering innovation and collaboration to contribute to the growth of Charleston, South Carolina as a thriving technology hub. The Harbor hack will be held in October over the first weekend: Friday, October 6- Sunday, October 8 2023

IN/F8 is on a mission to curate hackathon-accelerator events that inspire a fresh wave of talented individuals to explore and engage with the ever-evolving tech industry. Our ultimate goal is to establish a dynamic pipeline of innovative entrepreneurs who will launch cutting-edge tech startups, thereby driving the growth and development of Charleston, SC. Join us on this exciting journey, and let's collaborate to make a lasting impact!
