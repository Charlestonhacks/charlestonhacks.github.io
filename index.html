Charlestonhacks harborHack is a dynamic event where diverse individuals collaborate to innovate in a fast-paced and creative environment, unleashing problem-solving skills to develop innovative solutions to real-world problems.

It fosters community, out-of-the-box thinking, and celebrates the power of human ingenuity to make a positive impact. "harborHack" is open to hackers from all different backgrounds and skill levels,including developers, designers, business professionals, subject matter experts, designers, entrepreneurs, and students with an interest in technology, innovation, and social impact.
