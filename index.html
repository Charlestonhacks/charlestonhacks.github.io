CharlestonHacks is committed to creating immersive, transformational events and as our network grows, so does the impact of our events. Our mission is to create a pipeline of world-class software developers, entrepreneurs, and creative technologists. Our responsibility is to support them with our mentors, judges, speakers, facilitators, sponsors, employers, startup resources, and funding. Each event has a different focus, venue, and scale but everyone will develop prototypes, create materials to be presented, and pitch their ideas to our judges.
Create our culture, be inspired and inspire others, Seek opportunity and have a breakthrough.
Reach out to us to find out more about what we have in store for Charleston, South Carolina, in 2024.
