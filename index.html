CharlestonHacks mission is to help grow South Carolina into a thriving center for technology, entrepreneurship, and creative innovation.

To achieve this mission, we organize immersive and transformational events. These events are designed to bring together the diverse community of software developers, entrepreneurs, and creative technologists to grow our regional economy.

Each of our events is unique, featuring distinct focuses, venues, and scales. However, a common thread runs through all of them: participants will connect with a team of like-minded people, create prototypes, develop materials for presentation, and pitch their innovative ideas to our judges. Our aim is to be a resource for our community and organizations and to help drive progress, spark creativity, and support the next generation of leaders in technology, entrepreneurship, and the creative arts.


Create our culture, be inspired and inspire others, seek opportunity and have a breakthrough.


Reach out to us to find out more about what we have in store for Charleston, South Carolina, in 2024.
