CharlestonHacks mission is to help grow South Carolina into a thriving center for technology, entrepreneurship, and creative innovation.

To achieve this mission, we organize immersive, transformational events. These events are designed to bring together a diverse community of software developers, entrepreneurs, and creative technologists.

Each of our events is unique, featuring distinct focuses, venues, and scales. Participants will connect with a team of like-minded people, create prototypes, develop materials for presentation, and pitch their innovative ideas to our judges. In 2024 we will focus on being inspired and inspiring others, seeking opportunity, shaping our culture and having a breakthrough.


Reach out to us to find out more about what we have in store for South Carolina in 2024.
