CharlestonHacks is committed to creating immersive, transformational events for everyone involved, including our mentors, judges, speakers, facilitators, participants, sponsors and observers. As out network grows, so does our impact. Our mission is to create a pipeline of world class software developers, entrepreneurs, and creative technologists and to support there endeavors with local employers, and startup and funding resources. Our unique events are structured to make the most out of the limited time that competitors have to develop prototypes, create materials to be presented and pitch their ideas to our judges. Each event has a different focus, venue and scale. Reach out to us to find out more of what we have in store for Charleston South Carolina in 2024
