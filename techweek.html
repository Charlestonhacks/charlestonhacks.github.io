About the Festival

The 30 Second AI Film Fest is a 3-hour AI-generated video hackathon where participants will create innovative, AI-powered 30-second short films. Hosted by CharlestonHacks as part of Dig South and Charleston Tech Week, this event encourages creativity, experimentation, and storytelling through AI-generated content.

30-Second Impact – Our Theme

A great film isn't just action, it's a feeling. In just 30 seconds, craft a cinematic moment that captivates, moves, or surprises us and makes us feel something unforgettable. Whether it's awe, joy, or suspense, every frame must count. Use AI tools, whether for visuals, effects, or storytelling to push creative boundaries and craft a cinematic moment. No wasted time. No empty shots. Just pure impact.

Event Rules & Guidelines

Team Formation

Participants may compete solo or in teams of up to 5 people. Teams can be formed at the event or pre-arranged.

AI Usage & Tools

Films must be created entirely within the 3-hour event window. Teams must use at least one AI-powered tool like OpenArt, our featured AI film sponsor.

Film Requirements

Each film should be 30 seconds long and content must be PG-level. Films must tell a cohesive story or convey a clear concept.

Judging Criteria

Films will be evaluated based on Creativity & Innovation, Storytelling & Clarity, AI Integration, and Visual & Audio Quality.

Terms & Conditions

Eligibility: Open to all attendees of Charleston Tech Week. Participants must be 18+ or have parental consent.

Ownership & Rights: Teams retain full rights to their films but grant CharlestonHacks non-exclusive rights to showcase and promote them for marketing purposes.

Code of Conduct: Be respectful. Collaboration and knowledge sharing are encouraged, but plagiarism is prohibited. Pre-generated work is not allowed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need technical experience to participate?

No technical experience is necessary! Many AI tools are user-friendly and don't require coding knowledge. We'll provide tutorials and support during the event to help participants get started.

Can I use pre-existing footage or assets?

All content must be created during the 3-hour event window using AI tools. You cannot use pre-generated footage, but you may use royalty-free music from approved sources.

Will there be prizes?

Yes! We have exciting prizes for the top three films as well as category-specific awards. Prizes include cash awards, subscriptions to professional AI tools, and special recognition at the Charleston Tech Week closing ceremony.

How will the films be judged?

Films will be evaluated by a panel of industry experts based on creativity, storytelling, AI integration, and overall quality. There will also be an audience choice award determined by attendee votes.

Can I attend virtually?

While the main event is in-person at the College of Charleston, we do offer a virtual participation option. Remote participants will need to join our Discord server for the kickoff and submit their films through the same process.
