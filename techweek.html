Charleston, SC — LIGHTS, CAMERA, AI! CharlestonHacks is excited to announce the 30 Second AI Film Fest, an innovative event blending artificial intelligence, filmmaking, and creativity. This one-of-a-kind experience will take place on May 1st, 2025, from 7:00 PM to 10:00 PM, at the Simmons Center at the College of Charleston, as part of Dig South and Charleston Tech Week.

The 30 Second AI Film Fest challenges participants to craft short films of 30 seconds or less in just three hours using cutting-edge AI tools. These tools enable creators to transform simple text prompts into cinematic creations, making this event a unique fusion of technology and art.

AI Video Hackathon: Compete to create the most compelling AI-generated short film.

Networking Opportunities: Connect with innovators, industry leaders, and AI enthusiasts.

Inspiring Environment: Experience Charleston Tech Week’s dynamic community of creators and entrepreneurs.

Prizes and Recognition: Win exciting prizes and gain recognition for your creativity.

Whether you’re an AI developer, a storyteller, or someone curious about AI in filmmaking, this is your chance to innovate and shine. Register now to participate and join us in redefining the future of storytelling!
