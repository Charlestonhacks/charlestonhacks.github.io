harborHack (23) is over, but the journey continues! harborHack (23) was a great success! We had around 100 registrants submitting 14 projects in 1 weekend! The weekend began with an inspiring speech by Partic Bryant of the Harbor Entrepreneur Center and Code + Trust, followed by team formation. After everyone found a team we ended the night to get some rest fro the big "grind day" that followed. Participants worked from the moring and into the night working on a variety of inspired projects. The teams were truely motivated and were still working when the last mentor left! The final day, Sunday began with teams finishing up their presentations and submitting them to our platform. Many last minute adjustments were made but 14 teams pulled through and were able to present their prototypes. After the presentations, the judges deliberated in another room as Emma, Gavin and Amin presented to the audience. Judging was in and Lancie announced the winner of the honorable mention award "Water Watch, Dave announced the second place winner "StallWart" and Doug announced the first place team "HIVR". We are so proud of the amazing work everyone created at harborHack (23) and look forward to continue to support your endeavors in the future.

Impact > Input - the theme of harborHack (23), a beginning, the "ripple effect" of action setting in motion other actions as the impact becomes greater than the input. How will your project continue to amplify its mission after the hackathon is over?

Your idea - What matters to you? Is it sustainability, education, equality, or health? Perhaps you could create a Digital Mentor Platform, Community Energy Exchange, Food Waste Reduction App, Emergency Response Network, or a Learning Enrichment App. Work on something you really believe in; The best ideas will come from you!
