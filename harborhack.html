Harbor Hack

The harbor is the inspiring venue for our second hackathon.

Welcome to harborHack—an event that is about building a future where your project has effects beyond our event.
Our theme is the "Ripple Effect"-A hackathon where your actions set powerful events in motion. What's the Ripple Effect and why is it our theme? Imagine that the solution you create as circles emanating from a starting point. That first action effects other actions and your effort becomes more that what you started with.
Solutions that matter to you-What societal issues are important to you? Is it environmental sustainability, education, equality, or something unique to your perspective? Translate your passion into an innovative solutions that has the potential to have impact that extends beyond the event. Collaboration amplifies the ripple effect-Team up with change makers,designers, and visionaries who are all committed to making a solution more powerful and far-reaching than you could imagine on your own.
Your Code-Your code can touch lives and have an ongoing impact on society. Join us in shaping a world where innovation isn't just a means to an end—it's the starting point for a future we can all be proud of. Register here on devpost
