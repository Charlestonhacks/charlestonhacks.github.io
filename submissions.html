Lights, Camera, AI! – Official Rules & Terms


Event Overview The 30 Second AI Film Fest is a 3-hour AI-generated video hackathon where participants will create innovative, AI-powered 30-second short films. Hosted by CharlestonHacks as part of Dig South and Charleston Tech Week, this event encourages creativity, experimentation, and storytelling through AI-generated content. Date & Time: May 1st, 7:00–10:00 PM Location: Simmons Center, College of Charleston Rules 1. Team Formation Participants may compete solo or in teams of up to 4 people. Teams will be formed at the event or pre-arranged. 2. AI Usage & Tools Films must be created entirely within the 3-hour event window. Teams must use at least one AI-powered tool for video generation, editing, or enhancement (e.g., Runway, Sora, Pika, Kaiber, Stable Diffusion, DALL·E, ElevenLabs, ChatGPT, OpenArt etc.). A combination of traditional filmmaking techniques (e.g., live-action recording) and AI is permitted, but AI tools must play a significant role in the film's creation. 3. Film Requirements Each film must be 30 seconds but may be up to 2 minutes. The content must adhere to a general audience rating (PG-level: No explicit content, hate speech, or offensive material). Films must tell a cohesive story or convey a clear concept. AI-generated visuals, sound, and text are encouraged, but human storytelling and direction are key! 4. Submission & Presentation Films must be submitted before the 10:00 PM deadline via the provided submission form. All teams will present their films to the audience and judges for a live screening. 5. Judging Criteria Films will be evaluated based on: ✅ Creativity & Innovation – How original and engaging is the concept? ✅ Storytelling & Clarity – Does it deliver a compelling narrative? ✅ AI Integration – How effectively are AI tools used in the film’s creation? ✅ Visual & Audio Quality – Is the film well-produced with clear visuals and sound? 6. Prizes & Recognition Prizes will be awarded for Best Film, Most Creative Use of AI, and Audience Favorite. Winners will receive exclusive CharlestonHacks swag, tech prizes, and industry recognition. Terms & Conditions Eligibility Open to all attendees of Charleston Tech Week. No prior AI filmmaking experience required. Participants must be 18+ years old or have parental consent. Ownership & Rights Teams retain full rights to their films. However, by participating, you agree to grant CharlestonHacks and event partners non-exclusive rights to showcase and promote the submitted films for marketing or future events. Code of Conduct Be respectful to all participants, judges, and organizers. Collaboration and knowledge sharing are encouraged, but plagiarism is prohibited. Any attempt to pre-generate or submit work created before the event will result in disqualification.

30 Second AI Film Fest Submission

To streamline submissions and showcase all films in one place, we’re asking participants to upload their own videos to YouTube and tag them using our official event hashtag. .

How to Submit Your Film:

Upload your 30-second film to your own YouTube channel.

Include the event hashtag "#30saiff" in the video title or description.

Tag our official YouTube channel (@charlestonHacks-mc4ct) in your description.

Once uploaded, we’ll find your film via the hashtag and add it to our official festival playlist!
